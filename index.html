Simple & Effortless
How It Works
Step 1

Subscribe Online

Choose your plan and complete sign-up in under 2 minutes. One-time install included.

Step 2

We Install a Pipe

A permanent flush-mount pipe is buried in your lawn — flush with the ground, out of the way.

Step 3

Flag Goes Up

Our volunteers place your 3'×5' flag the day before each holiday. No effort required from you.

Step 4

We Pick It Up

The flag is collected the day after each holiday and stored safely until next time.
